Sketch Art empowers you to create your own experience and customize your drawing space/boxes by making pixelated paintings by moving your mouse or taps on the screen

Here is how-to:

  • Tap on the "Set Box" button and enter the number of boxes you want
  • Set your choice of color among the default gray, color picker or random color
  • Draw your thoughts by moving your mouse along paths/boxes. Mobile devices achieve this with just a tap on their screens
  • Move your mouse anti-clockwise to erase unwanted parts of the sketch or use the "Clear" button to erase all.

...And that's all! Go try it and feel the art!!
